## Design Context

### Users
Recruiters are the primary audience. They need to scan quickly, understand the portfolio owner's role and range, and move from first impression to project proof without friction. The site should also work for hiring managers and peers reviewing it on mobile.

### Brand Personality
Editorial, precise, calm. The interface should feel modern and technical without slipping into a generic developer aesthetic. Motion should feel confident and useful rather than decorative.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light-first Nova styling with neutral, slightly tinted surfaces and graphite text. Geist Mono is the main typeface, used with careful spacing and hierarchy so it feels premium. The site should feel like a minimal exhibition catalogue with mobile-app navigation cues.

### Design Principles
1. Make the first screen legible in seconds.
2. Let typography and spacing carry the brand before decoration does.
3. Keep motion directional and informative, especially for route changes.
4. Design mobile interactions as first-class, not scaled-down desktop behavior.
5. Use placeholder structure that is easy to personalize later without rewiring the layout.
